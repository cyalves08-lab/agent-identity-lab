# Ideal Clients

> Who we're for. MAURICE uses this to qualify leads and tailor messaging.

## Primary audience

Licensed insurance agents and financial advisors who:

- Are good at the work but **invisible in their market**.
- Compete on price because nothing else sets them apart.
- Want to be the **trusted authority**, not just another agent.
- Have a real story or conviction they've never turned into positioning.

## Segments by archetype

| If they are… | They map to | Lead with |
|--------------|-------------|-----------|
| Data-driven, serve high earners | Wall Street Strategist | Precision, proof |
| Local, family-focused | Community Authority | Trust, belonging |
| Faith / values led | Faith-Grounded Advisor | Integrity, conviction |
| Teach financial literacy | Urban Financial Educator | Education, access |
| Serve high-net-worth | Luxury Wealth Architect | Exclusivity, craft |
| Help executives in transition | Corporate Transition Expert | Clarity in change |

## Qualifying questions MAURICE can ask a lead

1. What do you sell most right now? (Term/Final Expense, IUL/Whole Life,
   Retirement/Annuities, Mortgage Protection, Business Solutions)
2. Who do you naturally connect with — young families, entrepreneurs, retirees,
   professionals?
3. What do you believe most agents in your industry get wrong?

These mirror the Agent Identity Lab™ interview, so a qualified lead is already
warmed up for the process.

## Not a fit

- Anyone seeking guaranteed income or returns (we don't promise outcomes).
- Anyone wanting tax or legal advice.
- Pure lead-gen shoppers with no interest in long-term brand.
