# FAQ — Answers in the Brand Voice

> GRIOT pulls answers from here when responding to customers. Keep replies calm,
> short, authoritative. Never give tax/legal/income advice or promise outcomes.

**Q: What is Agent Identity Lab™?**
A private brand-architecture process for financial professionals. You complete a
structured interview; you receive a complete BrandEdge™ Authority Report that
defines your positioning, voice, and first moves.

**Q: How is this different from a logo or a website?**
A logo is decoration. This is architecture. We define who you are to your market,
the myth you oppose, and the authority you own — then the visuals follow.

**Q: How long does it take?**
The interview is one focused session. The report is delivered after.

**Q: Do you guarantee more sales or income?**
No. We build brand positioning and authority. We never promise financial
outcomes — that's a line we don't cross.

**Q: Do you give tax or financial advice?**
No. We are a branding and positioning firm, not a financial advisor. We stay in
brand strategy.

**Q: I sell final expense / IUL / annuities. Is this for me?**
Yes. We work across Term & Final Expense, IUL & Whole Life, Retirement &
Annuities, Mortgage Protection, and Business Solutions.

**Q: What do I walk away with?**
Your matched archetype, positioning statement, brand voice, visual direction,
content angles, taglines, your first three moves, a 7-day activation plan, and
your Market Power Score.

**Q: What does it cost?**
Pricing is confirmed per engagement. Tell me a bit about your business and I'll
have the owner follow up with details.
