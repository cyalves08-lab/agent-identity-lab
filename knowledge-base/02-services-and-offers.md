## Core offer — Agent Identity Lab™

A guided brand-architecture process for one financial professional. The client
completes a structured deep-discovery interview; we return their complete
**BrandEdge™ Authority Report**.

**What's inside the BrandEdge™ Report:**

- Matched brand **archetype** + why it fits them specifically
- **Positioning statement** — "I help [audience] solve [problem] through [approach]"
- Executive brand summary & opportunity window
- Strength assessment + strategic gaps
- Signature beliefs & authority statement
- Brand-voice profile
- Visual identity direction (logo style, colors, typography, visual tone)
- Authority content angles
- Industry myth they oppose
- Elevated taglines
- First three moves to establish authority (action · why · consequence if ignored)
- 7-day authority activation plan
- **Market Power Score** (/10) + classification

## The six brand archetypes

The interview matches each client to one:

1. **Wall Street Strategist** — data-driven, high-income professionals.
2. **Community Authority** — local trust, families.
3. **Faith-Grounded Advisor** — values-based, faith communities.
4. **Urban Financial Educator** — financial literacy, first-gen wealth builders.
5. **Luxury Wealth Architect** — premium, high-net-worth.
6. **Corporate Transition Expert** — career transitions, executives.
