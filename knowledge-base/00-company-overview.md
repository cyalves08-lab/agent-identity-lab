# RevCentric Marketing — Company Overview

## What we do

RevCentric Marketing builds **institution-level brand positioning** for financial
professionals and service-based entrepreneurs. We help them stop competing on
price and start being chosen as the trusted authority in their market.

## Flagship product — Agent Identity Lab™

A private, structured **deep-discovery interview** that turns an agent's story,
beliefs, and market into a complete brand architecture. It runs in three phases:

1. **Identity** — why they do this work, what shaped their beliefs, how they
   communicate.
2. **Market** — exactly who they serve, the problem they want to own, the myth
   they oppose.
3. **Offer Direction** — what they're focused on selling and the outcome clients
   should reach.

## Deliverable — the BrandEdge™ Authority Report

At the end of the interview the client receives a full report: matched archetype,
positioning statement, executive brand summary, opportunity window, strengths and
strategic gaps, signature beliefs, authority statement, brand-voice profile,
visual identity direction, content angles, taglines, first three moves, a
7-day activation plan, and a Market Power Score (out of 10).

## Who we serve

Licensed insurance agents and financial advisors, primarily in:

- Term & Final Expense
- IUL & Whole Life
- Retirement & Annuities
- Mortgage Protection
- Business Solutions

## What we are NOT

- We do not give tax, legal, or income advice.
- We do not promise financial outcomes.
- We are a **branding and positioning** firm, not a financial advisor.
