## Proven winner — "Borrowed Authority" reels

**What it is:** take a reel that's already proven to grab attention (a trending or
viral clip), **reuse it**, and add your **own captions, framing, and spin** so it
carries *your* message. You're borrowing the attention the clip already earns and
pointing it at your brand.

**The proof:** on **June 14, 2026**, the `nspired_connections` account posted a
borrowed-authority reel — a **baby standing in front of a TV, worshiping** —
reused with the owner's own captions and spin. Results that day:
- **2,332 reached · 4,527 views · 625 interactions (373 likes, 46 comments)**
- That's roughly **4x** a normal strong day for that account. A mini-viral hit.

**Why it worked:** the visual is funny, human, and instantly stops the scroll. The
owner's caption gave it meaning and tied it to her voice. Attention first, message
second.

**How to repeat it (the recipe):**
1. Find a clip with a built-in "stop the scroll" hook — funny, sweet, surprising,
   relatable.
2. Reuse it and add the owner's own captions / text overlay / spin.
3. Tie it to one clear idea (a belief, a myth, an invitation) — not a hard sell.
4. Post it on the brand that's hot (`nspired_connections` first; test on others).
